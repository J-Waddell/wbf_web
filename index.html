PT-305 at the 28th Annual Wooden Boat Festival

The world’s only fully restored combat-veteran Pt boat in operation today!

The Lake Pontchartrain Basin Maritime Museum will host The National WWII Museum and their PT-305 boat at the Lightkeeper’s Cottage, on the grounds of the Museum, during the Wooden Boat Festival on Sunday, October 15th! Mark your calendar! Don’t miss this exciting opportunity! More details to follow.
